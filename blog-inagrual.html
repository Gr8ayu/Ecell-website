INAUGURAL CEREMONY

Event details

The Inaugural Ceremony of Cadbury Gifting presents E-summit 2019 Powered by Amazon Web Services started at 10:00am in the morning with the lighting of the lamp, with dignitaries such as Incharge Principal Dr. B Anand, Dean of student affairs Dr Shanmukha Nagaraj, Faculty Advisor Dr. N. S Narhari, Head of RV incubation center Dr. R K Manjunath, Chief Guest Mr. Abrar Basith and Trustee of RSST Ms Maya Chandra. The invocation song was sung by Shreya Mrigen of 6th Sem, IEM. This was followed by the welcome address by Dr. N. S Narhari, who described the relation of the guests with this college and welcomed them graciously.

The Convenors of the year, Aditya Somani and Mohit Jain came on stage to give a brief overview of the Summit and its events that lie ahead. Mr Abrar Basith was then called upon stage to address the gathering and he gave encouraging advice and a jovial touch to the entire event. Ms. Maya Chandra then followed with an inspirational speech that energised the crowd and made them realise that every talent has scope for development. A short address was given by the Incharge Principal, where he commended the efforts of the organizing committee and motivated the audience to participate in all the events to be held.

Following this, a very informative video of our Principal Dr. K N Subramanya was displayed on screen where he talked about the history of Entrepreneurship Development Cell, RVCE and how the incubation center will be beneficial to everybody. To end the event, The dean of student Affairs, Dr. Shanmukha Nagaraj gave the Vote of Thanks during which mementos were distributed to the Dignitaries. At 11:30am, the crowd was allowed to disperse and this marked a successful inaugural ceremony.
