ABOUT E-CELL

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) of RVCE, founded in 2007 stands tall as a student-run body with the goal of fostering the Entrepreneurial culture within the college campus, and beyond. The Cell stands out as a pioneering organization, having mentored myriad Startups over the years. We at E-Cell, also provide opportunities for our members to widen their network within the Entrepreneurial world through the many events and speaker sessions that we organize.

MISSION

Our mission of developing the change makers of the society is achieved by organizing seminars, competitions, workshops, conferences and these will impart the relevant skills to an entrepreneur, to anyone willing to take the step.

VISION

UNEARTHING TRUE POTENTIAL

We envision ourselves to be the cultivators of tomorrow’s leaders. Through the activities and workshops that we host, we give it our all to help people grow as successful managers and leaders; contributing to the growing startup ecosystem of India.

IDEATION IS OUR GENESIS

The Cell aims to make people realize the true sense and meaning of Entrepreneurship. The term is not only about creating a company, but is anything to do with innovating on your own idea

INCUBATE

We aim to convert the ideas of millennials into actual working startups by providing them with a platform to grow their business, constant guidance and immense opportunities throughout their journey

OUR INITIATIVES

E-Cell has always looked into helping the students in one way or the other,Following are the different initiatives from our side

Embark

Embark is held each year as the inaugural event of the cell. It’s an idea and innovation workshop open to all students of RVCE. Focuses on brainstorming, Idea Generation and Business-Plan creation. A panel of entrepreneurs have their own speaker sessions and declare the best business plan at the end of the event.




E-Summit

E-summit is an annual celebration of Entrepreneurship, having a footfall of over 8k. It is a summit for budding entrepreneurs, students, professionals, mentors, angel investors and venture capitalists.It includes talks from established entrepreneurs and a stunning array of competitions for students to showcase their talents.Workshops are conducted with respect to the current trends in the corporate market to help students be up to date with the times.

Gyaan Sessions

Every month, E-Cell invites Entrepreneurs from various ﬁelds to share and motivate the students of the Cell. Gyan Sessions were started with a goal to foster the entrepreneurial mindset within the students of the college. It’s with the help of events like these that students get to build their connections within the Startup ecosystem.



EXPEDIENS

Expedians is for the students of various engineering colleges to work with budding startups. It aims to collaborate with the Bangalore Entrepreneurship Cell and provide internships to all colleges that come under it. This will give students excellent exposure to how a startup functions and the responsibilities that accompany it.

RVCR

RVCR is our Campus Ambassador program that involves university students who work closely with the E-cell RVCE, to spread the spirit of entrepreneurship across the nation. The program offers vast opportunities to build networking skills and enhance public opinion of our ecell. RVCRs are benefited by this program as they are provided with an internship like experience, enabling them to receive guidance on how to invoke entrepreneurship amongst fellow milenials and receive a first hand experience of the events being conducted by our cell throughout the year.

Bangalore Entrepreneurship Challenge

The Bengaluru Entrepreneurship challenge is an inter-college initiative. It contains a set of entrepreneurship-related tasks involving creativity, teamwork and innovation. Tasks are strategically released over the period of one month. Teams are given tasks like interviewing entrepreneurs throughout the city, making statistical reports on the entrepreneurial ecosystem etc.
