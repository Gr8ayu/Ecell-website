Mock Ipl

Event details
Ipl Quiz

started at 12:45pm on 21st February. There were 25 questions in total out of which some were start marked (had more weightage). 39 teams sat for the quiz and 9 teams made it to the finals. The cutoff was 16 out of a total of 30 marks. The quiz took 30 minutes and there was a 10 minute run through. Later on the answers were released and then the names of the teams who qualified for the Bidding round. All this for over by 3pm.

Ipl Bidding

The bidding started at 4:30 where 9 teams were a part and each team was assigned a Junior Associate (1st year Volunteer) to take note of all the transactions. The bidding was done by Tawqeer, Prayank, Swapnil and Anurag. A total of 142 players were there and the design of the game was such that the mathematics had to be done in prior and the strategy must be such that the necessary structure was maintained. The design ensured only 3 teams were able to do that also one team was disqualified due to swapping of players several times.

The event got over by 8:30-9:00pm on 21st February. The faculty coordinators were present alongwith Esteemed Faculty Advisor Ecell, RVCE Dr. N S Narahari
