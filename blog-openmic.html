Open Mic

Open Mic was an event exclusively designed for providing students with a platform for showcasing their talents. It was an entertaining live show for helping talents flourish and gain recognition. Diversity in fields of interest such as music, literary work, dance and more was welcomed and appreciated.

Event details

Open Mic was held for 2 days of the 3-day entrepreneurial extravaganza - on the 22nd and 23rd of February, 2019. The event went on for roughly 3 hours each day, commencing around 5pm. Participants were given a Mic and the stage for around 300 seconds or 5 minutes to showcase their talent(s) in desired formats. An audience of 200-300 zealous individuals relished the event. Open Mic was held in collaboration with YourQuote, a reputed name in the creative social networking industry. 4 speakers graced the event throughout, namely Nitesh Jaiswal, Supriya, Mariyam and Malvika Narendra â€“ all creative writers belonging to YourQuote community. 25-30 individuals from R.V.C.E. and other colleges combined performed each day.

The event witnessed mesmerising addresses by budding as well as experienced writers, Stand-up comedian, musicians, dancers and poets.

The general response for the event was positive and appreciative. All participants enjoyed presenting their views and talents. The audience was jubilant and an overwhelming response during and after the event was obtained.
