BUSINESS MARATHON

Business marathon is one of the flagship events that happened during Esummit 19 at RV college of Engineering. The event goes on the lines of a hackathon but instead of being programming oriented, the event goes more on the lines of ideating over a business model and an entrepreneurship idea. It is an overnight event and is conducted in 2 rounds. In the duration of the event hours, the teams built up a business model from scratch based on the guidelines from the event organizers and the problem statements allotted to them which were given by the partner of the event which is Amazon Web Services (AWS).

Event details

40 teams consisting from 5 to 6 members each were allowed to participate. The event started around 8:45 PM on 22nd February. The zero round involved the announcement, explanation and the bidding for the problem statements. This round was concluded by the announcement of the results of bidding at 9:30 PM. Right after the allocation of problem areas, the next task involved teams to come up with a detailed problem and a brief solution theyâ€™ll be working on for the entirety of the event. This round lasted for another 1.5 hours after which they spoke about their ideas for 6 minutes each. Right after that followed a break till 3:15 PM.

At 3:15 AM the teams started working on their ideas. They were introduced to Lean Canva Business model on which they were supposed to build their ideas upon and the platform Trello which was used to implement Lean Canva digitally. The teams worked on their ideas till 6:30 in the morning which concluded the end of first round of Business Marathon.

The finals of the event began at 12:30 PM on 23rd February. In this round the teams explained their business model in front of the judges. Each team was given a time of 10+5 mins(10 minutes for explanation and 5 for questions) . The finals for Business Marathon went on till 3:30 PM.

Finally the event was concluded with the announcement of the results by the judges. The winning team was team no. 4 which was comprised by third years of RVCE. The runner up team was team no 33 which was comprised by 4th year RVCE students.
