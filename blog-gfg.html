GeeksforGeeks Workshop

Event details

The GeeksForGeeks Workshop was successfully organised on 22nd February in the New Sports Complex, in association with GeeksForGeeks Organisation. The event started at 5:30PM, and around 280 students took part in the workshop.The workshop was conducted in New Sports Complex after dure verification of every registered candidate.

At 5:40 PM ,all the candidates settled at their respective places and the volunteers handed them A4 sheets for taking notes during the workshop. This was followed by the welcoming on the stage of Mr. Sandeep Jain, Founder of GeeksForGeeks . Mr. Jain , a coding enthusiast and a prolific name in the competitive coding verse has an experience in his domain for 20+ years. Mr. Jain started his workshop by giving a brief introduction about himself and how his inclination towards competitive coding grew. He also explained how he managed convert a simple coding blog into a fully developed coding platform, GEEKSFORGEEKS.

At 6:00PM , he finished with his introduction and started with the workshop. The topics he covered in the workshop were carefully curated to deliver the right things to the appropriate crowd consisting mainly of 1st year students. The workshop covered topics like Data Structures, Algorithms, Dynamic Programming , competitive approach towards coding questions and optimisation of codes. At 6:45 PM , he opened the house for questions and cleared doubts of students. He answered questions using simple anecdotes , which could be easily understood by everyone. He took many questions from participants and faculty coordinators alike and answered them with utmost sincerity. At 7:30 PM , the workshop ended . The feedback received from the participants as well as the organising delegates, was largely positive and constructive.
